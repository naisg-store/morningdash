Legal & Privacy

Privacy Policy

How we respect your device data and location privacy.

Location Data Usage

Morning Weather Brief uses your browser's Geolocation API solely to fetch relevant weather forecasts for your immediate area. Your coordinates are processed locally in client-side JavaScript and are never stored on external databases or tracked across the web.

Cookies & Local Storage

We do not use tracking cookies. We utilize standard browser localStorage to store weather forecast data for up to 15 minutes. This minimizes network calls, saves device battery, and ensures instant loading when you reopen the page.

Third-Party Services & Ads

We use third-party advertising partners, including Google AdSense, to display non-intrusive ads. Google may use cookies or device identifiers to serve ads based on your visit to this and other websites. You can manage your personalized ad preferences through Google's Ad Settings.
