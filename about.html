About The Project

Morning Weather Brief

Streamlined, zero-friction morning weather metrics tailored for daily commuters.

Our Mission

Morning Weather Brief was built to solve a simple morning annoyance: traditional weather apps are cluttered with heavy ads, slow video loops, and unnecessary clicks when all you want to know is what conditions look like before heading out the door.

By aggregating open meteorological data and processing localized decision logic directly in your browser, Morning Weather Brief delivers an instant overview of temperature, humidity, wind conditions, rain likelihood, and highway commute hazards.

Data Sources & Performance

Weather metrics are powered by Open-Meteo's open-source forecasting API, updated automatically every 15 minutes and cached locally on your device to keep performance instant, private, and lightweight.
